Difficulty

  • Easy - 100% more drops, 50% enemy stats.
  • Medium - same levels as the regular game.
  • Hard - 50% less items found, 50% less drops, 100% higher enemy stats.
  • Very hard - 80% less items found, 80% less drops, 200% higher enemy stats.
  • Even harder - 95% less items, 95% less drops, 300% enemy stats.

Preset by purpose

  • Any% challenge - Equips and creatures randomness is higher, so some may be difficult to get.. difficulty easy, random equips increased, creatures crazy, progressiveness increased. spawn rate medium, amplitude absolute-anywhere, style random position. key drops Fire World, Ilusionists illusion-world
  • 100% challenge - Equips have high chance to drop. Enemies have high chance to spawn. difficulty easy, random equips increased, creatures increased, progressiveness medium. spawn rate predictable, amplitude within-world, style random position. key drops Ashen Cavern, Illusionists worship-area
  • Scary Game - Highest randomness and unpredictability. difficulty hard, random equips crazy, creatures crazy, progressiveness flat. spawn rate crazy-king-hopper-like, amplitude absolute-anywhere, style crazy-may-not-work. key drops anywhere, illusionists anywhere
  • Stable - Randomizes mostly by shuffling. difficulty medium, random equips only-swap, creatures only-swap , progressiveness flat. spawn rate only-swap, amplityde anywhere-within-world, style only-swap. key drops keep on cerberus, illusionists no-change
  • Comedy - Creatures lifted on air. Drop pitcher of Nadya filled up. Two or more creatures spawns same position. Doors removed. Set everything to crazy config. Equips drop much increased. Lot of drop Nadya jars and keys in the beginning.
  • Bonanza - Plenty of nice drops very soon in the game.
  • Only fix King Hopper - Only fix rare spawns. Change low chance spawns into 100% spawn chance.
  • Only directives - Doesn't randomize or change the game order, creatures. Only "fix" for completion, applying directives. Make possible to complete game 100% after one play through without having to mule equips using vs mode into new game. As downside, also apply the directive to havo no HP or MP recovery in the game.

Map randomization

  • Untouched - Keep world areas.
  • Shuffled - Shuffle all areas.
  • Increased - May have contradictory doors where returning may not go back but go somewhere instead.

Random equips stats

  • Soft - from -10% to +10% the average of the area.
  • Medium - from -30% to +100% the average of the area.
  • Increased - from -70% to +300% the average of the area.
  • Crazy - from -99% to +3000% the average of the area.

Random creature stats

  • Soft - from -10% to +10% the average of the area.
  • Medium - from -30% to +100% the average of the area.
  • Increased - from -70% to +300% the average of the area.
  • Crazy - from -99% to +3000% the average of the area.

Spawn rate

    Consider directive "To remove randomness" overwrites this option if enabled.
  • Predictable - Creatures spawn very easy.
  • Medium - Same level as the game, but no exceptional rare cases like King Hopper.
  • Crazy like King Hopper - Many enemies may be very rare to spawn like the King Hopper.

Random spawn style

Crazy - Creatures may end up in walls or cause more strangeness and surprises.

Progressiveness

  • Flat - Po progressiveness, all areas have the same level of difficulty, that is the game average.
  • Medium - Similar to the game progressivity: every area goes about 10% harder than previous.
  • Increased - Game starts 3x easier and ends 3x harder. Recommended to enhance the game positive feedback on collecting items in early game.
  • Crazy - Progressiveness is chaotic, each area is decided to be from 10x easier to 10x harder individually.

Fiery key / Flaming key drop

In regular game, Fiery key is dropped by Cerberus, then used to open a compartment where is Flaming key. Flaming key is used to teleport to the place where the Ebony Knight is. As one of the world bosses, Ebony Knight should be defeated in order to be able to enter the final room and beat the game. So the Fiery key is needed for achieving end of the game.

Flaming key in the first area - Makes easier game. You don't need to collect Fiery key. Just collect Flaming key in early game and use it in Ashen cavern to find Ebony Knight.

Ilusionists

Ilusionists are Lord Gorthur, Jester and Willowess, need to be killed in order to make Illusion World boss Disguise to show up. In the regular game they are in the worship area.

To remove randomness so it will be total deterministic

Every creature spawns soon and drops always the same at 100% chance.

All equips

Possibility to complete all items in one game play. It makes much more equips to be dropped, so as consequence you'll have much more, durability will be reduced and will have less drops of consumables.

No HP or MP recovery

To force good use of equipments and management of HP and MP. Eliminate practice of waiting to fill up HP and MP. Instead, there will be more availability of HP and MP potions and dorado ashes.

Randomization info

Advanced knowledge of the game is not required.

The preset options set the other options to level where it provides reasonable play time for the expected experience. Recommended to not change other parameters other than difficulty.

"Only swap" option for each parameter makes no random generation but just swapping, so it is guarenteed that you don't get weird numbers, may be the safer way to play if the randomness gets non-reliable.

From the recipe it is generated a changeset file (this is the one to be given to other people in order for them to reproduce the exact same output without knowing the spoilers). It can be uploaded for applying to iso again. When it applies, it's checksum is also applied to display in the initial short sword description.

Randomization produces a intermediary Recipe, that contains the readable information of all random set that are intended to be performed. You can also download, change and upload it to do your own customized changes in the game.

I expect to implement also enemy walk speed and attack cooldown time randomization, as well as capability of adding more creatures spawn and items found than the regular game, but as additional features since these seem to be more complex to do.

Game play

You need to verify equipment and equips stats in game, as they may differ from regular game.

Mind that with randomized areas, you should manage exploration like you do when playing for first time.

To beat the game, you still need to kill all world bosses. They are in their original areas. For Desguise to spawn you still need to defeat the ilusionists first.

Bosses and event-related creatures are not randomized.
